## Brand Voice Guidelines

### Do
- Write like you're explaining something fascinating to a smart friend
- Use clear structure 芒聙聰 headings, short paragraphs, visual breaks
- Reference real examples, history, or personal experience
- Be honest about uncertainty 芒聙聰 "I think" is allowed
- Mix long-form depth with short punchy observations

### Don't
- Use jargon without explanation
- Write in a corporate or marketing tone
- Oversimplify to the point of losing meaning
- Lecture or condescend
- Chase trends for engagement
